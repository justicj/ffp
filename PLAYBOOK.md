# 🏈 Flag Football Playbook

7-on-7 Spread Flag Football

---

## Roster

| Label | Role | Default Alignment |
|-------|------|-------------------|
| Q | Quarterback | Shotgun, 7 yards behind center |
| C | Center | Line of scrimmage (center) |
| X | #1 Receiver | Right wide |
| Z | #2 Receiver | Left wide |
| Y | Slot Receiver | Right slot |
| H | Hybrid Receiver/Blocker | Left slot |
| R | Running Back/Blocker | Backfield, left of Q |

## Default Formation

```
  Z    Y   [C]   H    X      ← Line of Scrimmage
              ║
         R    Q              ← 7 yards back
```

> ⚠️ Defense can rush from the 1-yard line. **Every play has a hot read.**
